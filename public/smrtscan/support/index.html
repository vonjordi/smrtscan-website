Scanning & receipts

How does scanning work?

Point your camera at a receipt and SMRTscan captures it, then reads it automatically — pulling out the merchant, date, total, GST/HST, and individual line items. The extracted receipt opens on a detail screen where you can review and adjust anything before saving.

A receipt didn't scan or extracted the wrong details

Accuracy depends on the photo, so for best results:

  • Get the whole receipt in frame, including the top (merchant) and bottom (totals)
  • Lay it flat and avoid shadows, glare, and creases
  • Use even, bright lighting — faded thermal receipts scan better with more light

You can always tap any field on the receipt detail screen to correct it by hand. Your edits are saved and used as-is for totals and tax, so a wrong scan never means re-doing everything.

Can I add a receipt I already have as a photo or PDF?

Yes — alongside live camera capture you can bring in an existing image of a receipt, and it runs through the same extraction. If something doesn't import the way you expect, send us the file and we'll look into it.

Is there a limit on how many receipts I can scan?

Your plan includes a monthly allowance of AI-powered scans. If you reach it, you can still add and edit receipts manually, and the allowance resets at the start of your next billing period. Your exact allowance is shown in the app on the subscription screen.

Taxes & deductions

How does SMRTscan handle GST/HST and my tax position?

As you scan, SMRTscan captures the GST/HST on each receipt and rolls it into your overall tax position so you can see your input tax credits and deductible spending add up over time, rather than tallying it manually at year-end.

What is SMRTaudit?

SMRTaudit reviews your receipts and flags things worth a second look — missing details, possible duplicates, or entries that may need attention — so your records are clean and defensible before you file or hand them to an accountant.

What is the deduction guide?

The deduction guide helps you understand which expenses are typically deductible and how to categorize them, so more of your eligible spending actually makes it onto your return. It's guidance to help you organize — not a substitute for advice from a qualified tax professional.

How does mileage tracking work?

The mileage tracker lets you log business trips and keeps a running record you can use toward vehicle deductions, kept in one place alongside your receipts.

Documents, accounting & export

What are PDF documents?

Beyond individual receipts, SMRTscan can keep PDF documents — invoices, statements, and other records — organized in the same place, so everything for your books lives together.

How do I export my receipts and reports?

You can export your receipts and PDF documents from within the app to share with your accountant or keep your own copy. If you need a full export of your data in a specific format, email us and we'll help.

Can I share data with my accountant or team?

SMRTscan supports workspaces so a business's receipts and documents can be kept together and shared with the right people. If you're moving from a personal setup to a shared workspace, or inviting someone, and run into trouble, send us a note — we can help sort it out.

Account, subscription & privacy

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed through Apple. Open the iOS Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → SMRTscan to change or cancel. Cancelling stops future renewals; you keep access until the end of the current period.

How do I restore a purchase on a new device?

Sign in with the same Apple ID you subscribed with, then use Restore Purchases in the app's settings. If your subscription doesn't reappear, email us and we'll get it sorted.

Is my data private?

SMRTscan is built privacy-first. See our Privacy Policy for exactly what's collected and how it's handled.

How do I delete my account and data?

Email us from the address on your account and we'll permanently delete your account and associated data. You can also review our Terms of Service for details on your account.
